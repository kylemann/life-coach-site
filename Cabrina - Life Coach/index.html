What is Life Coaching?

We help you develop realistic and attainable goals that create progress in your personal and/or professional life. The first session is to examine a general challenge, obstacle to overcome and intended goal.

The clients goal is to record "triggers" that prevent progress and to also write "moments" of progress to share during future sessions. Sessions vary based on the situation, motivation and drive of the client. Appointments are conducted live,via Skype and/or phone calls.
