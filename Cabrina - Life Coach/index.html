What is Life Coaching?

We help you develop realistic and attainable goals that create progress in your personal and/or professional life. The first session is to examine a general challenge, obstacle to overcome and intended goal. The clients goal is to record "triggers" that prevent progress and to also write "moments" of progress to share during future sessions. Sessions vary based on the situation, motivation and drive of the client. Appointments are conducted live,via Skype and/or phone calls.

Cabrina McLain is the life coach, mentor, business executive and community volunteer. She has worked for 4 major companies and has been trained by award winning consultants to conduct effective team building classes, create action plans to develop a healthy work culture and has taught to help clients identify the root cause of a problem to ultimately drive change and results.

Cabrina's first goal to make sure that her clients feel comfortable with the process and are willing to work to achieve a goal.
