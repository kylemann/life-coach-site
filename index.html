What we do

As your coach, we help you develop realistic and attainable goals that generate progress in your personal and professional life. It begins with a conversation to examine your challenges or obstacles and then we create goals toward change.

The client's goal is to record triggers that prevent progress and to also write moments of progress to share during future sessions. Sessions vary based on the situation and motivation of the client. Appointments are offered live, via Skype or on the phone.

Our consultants help improve individuals and companies by evaluating weaknesses and recommending solutions. We take the time to learn, assess and create smart action plans to help every client drive results.

We strive for clients to open up to a new way of implementing change and embracing a "new experience" which will help them achieve their goals.

Meet Coach


Cabrina

Cabrina McLain is a life coach, mentor, business executive and community volunteer. Cabrina has over twenty years of experience in the retail and hospitality fields as a Learning and Development Specialist, Hotel General Manager, and as a HR/Operations Executive. She has worked for 4 major Fortune 500 companies and has been trained by award winning consultants. Her areas of expertise involves: conducting effective team building classes, creating action plans to develop a healthy work culture, and helping clients identify the root cause of a problem to ultimately drive change and results.

Cabrina's first goal to make sure that her clients feel comfortable with the process and have the will to work to achieve a goal. She is known for her relaxed yet direct approach and has partnered with her clients to create a plan that resolves challenges. Cabrina has a team of coaches from all over the country that contribute to individualized and special needs of every client.
